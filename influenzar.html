Frequently Asked Questions

How are commissions calculated?

10% of your referred client's first invoice with Bestpen — that's it. No formulas. No sliding scales. Refer someone who pays ₦200,000? Your cut is ₦20,000, paid within 72 hours.

When do I get paid?

Within 72 hours of your referred client clearing their invoice. We pay via direct bank transfer. Minimum payout is ₦10,000.

Who can join Influenzar?

Creators, coaches, freelancers, social media managers, agency owners — anyone with a network that includes businesses.

Is there a limit to how much I can earn?

Zero. Refer 1 client or 100 clients — commission applies to every single first invoice.

What if my referral forgets to use my code?

Email us within 48 hours with proof of your introduction. We review it manually. If it checks out, we credit your commission.
